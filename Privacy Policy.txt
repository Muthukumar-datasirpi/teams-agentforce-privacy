Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This integration app (“Agentforce AI”) connects Microsoft Teams with Salesforce Agentforce to allow users to access Salesforce data and insights directly within Teams.

Data Collection and Usage:

The App accesses basic user information from Microsoft Teams and Salesforce (for example, user ID, name, and messages related to Salesforce records).

No personal data is permanently stored by the developer. All API requests are processed directly between Microsoft and Salesforce.

Data is used only to enable communication between Teams and Salesforce and is not shared with any third parties.

Data Storage and Security:

No data is stored on external servers.

Microsoft Teams and Salesforce handle all authentication and encryption per their respective security policies.

User Rights:
If you wish to remove or disconnect the integration, you can do so anytime from Microsoft Teams settings or Salesforce connected apps.
